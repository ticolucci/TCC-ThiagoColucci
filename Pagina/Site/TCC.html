Evaluation of Web Service Compositions in Large Scale

 
 

    This work will assist in developing the project Baile, Institute of Mathematics and Statistics, University of São Paulo (IME-USP) in partnership with Hewlett-Packard (HP) of the United States, as well as project CHOReOS, IME-USP in partnership with a European consortium.

    Baile’s main goal is to study the problems related to the development of choreography in large scale environments, particularly in the context of Cloud Computing. The ultimate goal of CHOReOS, for USP will be to implement a Service Oriented Middleware for the "Internet of the Future", an Ultra-Large Scale internet, heterogeneous and dynamic.

    This work will also help in studying the problems for Baile and also show how scalable the current techniques of choreography and orchestration are, helping CHOReOS to understand the current state of the art of these techniques of composition of web services.
